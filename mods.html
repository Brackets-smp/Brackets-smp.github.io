Mods

Welcome to Brackets !

Brackets is a modded SMP which runs on Fabric.
The download link is at the bottom of the page. It takes you to the GitHub releases page of the modpack.
You can find a MultiMC modpack (which includes the configs) as well as all the mods in a zip.

Installation (for the mods.zip users):

Copy the mods in your mod folder (.minecraft/mods), then start your game.

Notable mods:

  • Terralith, Incendium, Nullscape, Structory (& Structory Towers): world generation mods
  • YUNG's Better Structures mods
  • Friends&Foes: adds back the mobs that were voted out in the mob votes
  • Farmerâ€™s Delight: improves on the farming and food aspects of the game
  • AdventureZ: new bosses
  • AdditionZ: tweaks and new enchantments
  • Creeper Overhaul: creepers blend in their environment
  • Supplementaries: decoration blocks that are also functionnal
  • FallingTree: chop trees instantly with an axe, it removes durability accordingly to the number of logs in the tree
  • Trinkets: allows you to put multiple things in your chestplate slot, allowing for a chestplate with elytra
  • Dropz: item physics
  • Many mods from Fuzs: Universal Bone Meal, Block Runner, Diagonal Fences (it fits nicely imo), Easy Anvils, Easy Shulker Boxes, Easy Magic, Visual Workbench, and possibly more that i forgot
  • And many mods for performance, sound and immersion improvements(with Auditory, Presence Footsteps, ExtraSounds, AmbientSounds, Sound Physics Remastered, and Drip Sounds for the sound part)
