Rules :

General rules :

  • Steal and grief aren’t allowed.
  • Claims in #claims. People are prohibited from doing anything in your claims unless you tell them.
  • PVP is not allowed until your opponent agrees to fight.
  • You can't have more than 2 elytras (if the first breaks, for example).
  • Don’t kill animals often near spawn (they respawn slowly, just make a farm).
  • Cheats which give you an unfair advantage are not allowed (mods like X-Ray or Freecam).
  • Cheat clients like Wurst are not allowed.
  • Seed mappers are forbidden. If you leak the seed you'll be banned.
  • Traps are not allowed (be nice to each other).

Shops rules :

  • Don’t steal the resources of the shops.
  • Selling elytras is forbidden.
  • Respect the prices.
