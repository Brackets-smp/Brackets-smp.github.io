Mods

Welcome to Brackets !
Brackets is a modded SMP which runs on Fabric.
There is a download link at the bottom of the page.
It will take you to the GitHub's releases page, where you can download the mods in a .zip or in a MultiMC instance.
Ignore the MultiMC instance if you don't use this launcher or one of its forks (like Prism Launcher).

Installation:
Copy the mods in your mod folder (.minecraft/mods), then start your game

Optional:
Close your game, and after that copy the configs in your config folder (.minecraft/config).

Notable mods:

  • - Advanced Netherite: adds some new tiers of netherite, you combine the pieces with netherite ingots mixed with the other ores to make netherite-iron pieces forexample, or netherite-gold, etc.
  • - Create: the famous Create mod, which adds lots of redstone machines to make cool contraptions
  • - Charm: this adds vanilla-like features which are nice to have
  • - Axes Are Weapons: turns the axes in weapons because by default if you attack with an axe it'll remove 2 points of durability to it, makes it so it only removes 1
  • - Better Than Mending: adds the ability to sneak-right-click a Mending item to repair it with experience you already have
  • - Crops Love Rain: makes crops and saplings grow faster while it's raining
  • - Fabrication: some tweaks to the game
  • - Leaves be gone: fast leaf decay
  • - Plasmo Voice: an in-game proximity voice chat
  • - Nicer portals: make the portal any shape you want. You can also make the frame out of crying obsidian
  • - Trade cycling: cycle villager trades with the click of a button (in the villager's trading interface)
  • - Universal Graves: adds gravestones that keep your items upon dying
  • - Universal Enchants: allows for mixing enchants for tools, armor and weapons, for example Infinity + Mending, Looting on an axe, Quick Charge on bows, etc.
  • - And a lot of QoL and optimisation mods!
